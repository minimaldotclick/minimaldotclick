About minimal.click

MINIMAL.CLICK an experimental small-tech, self-sufficient creative research studio

It has been created to explore code, tools, games, music, writing, and living using sustainable and ethical business practices through the utilisation of open source / FOSS tools, philosophies and methodologies.

Will this small creative studio be able to offset my cost of living, make me a production house of one, help me tread lighter upon the earth?

This is what minimal.click is for. To find out if this is possible.

The plan for this wiki is to host research into open source tools, methodologies, gamedev, sustainability, music and more. If the tools do not exist then I should attempt to make my own.

- research open source tools
- research open source methodologies
- research into creativity
- research into play
- research into games
- research into storytelling
- research into games as art
- research into sustainable one man production shop
- research into self-sufficiency / self-reliance
- research into analogue and digital sustainability
- research into can I do this as a sustainable business
- research into the five excellences

This research studio is run by Malcolm Content, an old punk trying to reconnect with his core values. Everything is a work in progress.
